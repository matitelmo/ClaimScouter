How It Works

  1. First Time: When you click "Open Funnel", it will start from step 1
  2. After Completion: Once you complete the funnel (reach step 5), it's marked as completed in localStorage
  3. Subsequent Visits: When you open the funnel again, it will skip directly to step 5 (thank you page)
  4. Reset: Use the "Reset Funnel Completion" button to clear the completion status and start fresh
