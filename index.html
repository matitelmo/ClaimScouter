Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Claim Scouter

Is my data secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use bank-level AES-256 encryption and partner with Plaid for secure bank connections. We never see or store your banking passwords, and all connections are read-only.

What information do you access?

We only access the information necessary to find claims: email receipts and transaction history. We use Plaid to connect to your bank, which means we never see your banking credentials.

Do you sell my data?

Never. Your data is only used to find claims you're eligible for. We have a strict privacy policy and never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

How do you find claims?

Our automated system continuously monitors your purchase history against our comprehensive database of active class action settlements. We scan email receipts and transaction data to identify matches, then use Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to automatically file claims on your behalf.

How long does the process take?

Initial claim scanning takes 24-48 hours. Once we find claims, you can approve them with one click. Payouts typically arrive within 30-90 days, depending on the settlement timeline.

Do I need to do anything after signing up?

No! Once you connect your accounts, we handle everything automatically. We'll notify you when we find claims, and you just need to approve them. We file all the paperwork and track the process for you.

What types of settlements do you cover?

We cover a wide range of class action settlements including data breaches, consumer fraud, defective products, antitrust cases, and more. We monitor both active and upcoming settlements.

How much money can I expect?

Payouts vary widely depending on the settlement. Some claims are worth $5-25, while others can be hundreds of dollars. The average user recovers $50-150 across multiple settlements. Remember, we guarantee to find at least $48 in available claims in your first year or we refund your $24 fee.

Will this affect my credit score?

No, filing claims for class action settlements does not affect your credit score. These are not loans or credit applications - they're simply claiming money you're already owed.

Can I disconnect my accounts later?

Yes, you can disconnect your accounts at any time through your dashboard. However, keeping them connected allows us to continue monitoring for new claims on your behalf.

How do I update my payment information?

You can update your payment information anytime in your account settings. We'll use your updated payment method for all future payouts.

Can I cancel my account?

Yes, you can cancel your account at any time. If we've already filed claims on your behalf, you'll still receive any payouts you're owed, minus our success fee.
